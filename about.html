Restaurant

Restaurants originated a long time ago on the main roads to serve the passengers and provide food and shelter for money and then developed and spread in all streets and areas.

The restaurant serves a great service. I knew its value when I was away from home and hungry and entered a simple restaurant that I did not know and had never heard of before.

I was tired and in need to recover my strength and rest from the sun heat. I sat down and ordered cold juice. The glass of juice was large and full of ice cubes. I needed this cup. It is the most delicious juice I have ever tasted.
